How It Works

How Our Platform Works

A simple, automated system that connects merchants with marketers to deliver more sales with zero effort. Here's how the process works from start to finish:

1

Merchant Uploads Products

Merchants add their products with pricing, and the system makes them available for marketers to promote.

2

Marketer Promotes Landing Page

Each marketer receives a unique landing page link to promote the merchant’s products online.

3

Customer Places an Order

The customer submits their information through the landing page, and the order is automatically linked to the right marketer.

4

Shipping & Payments

The shipping company delivers the product to the customer and transfers payments to both the merchant and the marketer automatically.
